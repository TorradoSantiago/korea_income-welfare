# Executive Brief

## In one sentence

Machine-learning case on Korean welfare microdata focused on the relationship between education and income.

## What problem it addresses

It measures how much signal education provides when trying to explain income differences, while making clear that education alone does not explain everything.

## What a non-technical reader can see

- A large dataset.
- Charts and model comparisons.
- A credible conclusion: education matters, but it is not the whole story.

## What it demonstrates

- Data preparation and modeling.
- Quantitative reasoning with honest interpretation.
- Strong fit for analytics, applied economics, and consulting positioning.
